❓ Frequently Asked Questions

📌 How do I register as a job seeker? ▼
Click "I need a job" on the home page, fill in your details, upload your CV and photo. Registration is FREE. We will contact you within 24 hours with matching job opportunities.
📌 How much does it cost to post a job? ▼
Job posting costs UGX 100,000 per post. You can request a quote via WhatsApp first. Payment is made to Airtel +256 709 937 339 or MTN +256 783 168 227.
📌 What is the 14-day free replacement guarantee? ▼
If a staff member leaves or doesn't meet expectations within 14 days of hire, we replace them at NO additional cost. Your initial fee remains as credit.
📌 How do I become a promoter or broker? ▼
Click "Promoters (16%)" or "Brokers (40%)" in the menu, register with your details, and receive your unique code. Share the code to earn commissions.
📌 What is the Revenue Growth Program? ▼
We offer three consulting plans: Starter (UGX 300k/mo), Professional (UGX 800k/mo), and Elite (UGX 1.5M/mo). Includes upselling training, menu engineering, weekly consultations, and guest retention systems.
📌 How do I apply for a job? ▼
Browse available jobs in the "Jobs" section, click "Apply now", pay UGX 10,000 application fee, and submit your details. We will connect you with the employer.
